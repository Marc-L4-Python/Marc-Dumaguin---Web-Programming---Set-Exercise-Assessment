PLOT

   When the ecosystem collapses in 2067, worldwide hunger poses a threat to human survival. During a dust storm, farmer and former NASA astronaut Joseph Cooper finds a gravitational anomaly with his daughter Murph. This takes them to a covert NASA facility, where they discover there is an ongoing mission to use a wormhole close to Saturn to find a habitable planet. Cooper reluctantly leaves his family behind and joins a team of scientists to pilot the Endurance spacecraft, which includes Dr. Brand and his daughter Amelia.

   Upon exiting the wormhole, the crew encounters several challenges, among them being time dilation. On the first planet, they lose a member and watch as 23 years go by in a matter of hours. Murph, who is growing up on Earth during all of this, is trying to solve the equation that will allow humans to leave.

   On the second planet, they meet Dr. Mann, who deceives them and jeopardizes their mission. Romilly dies, and the Endurance sustains damage. After Cooper and Amelia make a risky detour around the black hole, another 51 years go by.

   Cooper becomes trapped in a four-dimensional tesseract and uses time travel to communicate with Murph in order to give her crucial information. When he returns, humanity has flourished on a new colony. On the third planet, Cooper and TARS meet up with Amelia and start over, leaving Earth and its memories behind.
